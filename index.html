About Us

CodeZeo is a London-based technology studio dedicated to crafting innovative, reliable, and privacy-first applications for a global audience. Our mission is simple: build smart software that empowers people, gets out of their way, and always respects their data.

Founded with a passion for clean design and user-centric development, we focus on creating products that are as intuitive as they are powerful. Every line of code we write is guided by our core principles: simplicity, security, and scalability.

  • Revenue Model: Simple and transparent monthly & yearly subscription plans – no hidden fees.
  • Infrastructure: Powered by the App Store ecosystem, integrated with RevenueCat for seamless subscription management, and privacy-friendly analytics to ensure user trust.
  • Data Privacy: We never sell or misuse your data. All analytics are anonymized and GDPR-compliant.
  • Support: Dedicated and responsive support team, contact us anytime at info@codezeo.com.
