FAQ

Is there a limit on how many drinks I can log?

When you subscribed, you have access to all features.

How does CoffZeo calculate caffeine?

We use a standard calculation algorithm to estimate how much caffeine in drinks, and you can adjust your personal limits in Settings.

Does it sync with Apple Health?

We're currently working on this feature for the closest updates.

Does CoffZeo work offline?

Yes. All your logs and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Can I add custom drinks?

Currently, all common drinks including caffeine are listed as an option. In a close update, users can create custom presets with specific caffeine amounts for their favorite brews.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

Your data stays on your device. So, if you delete the app, your local data will be deleted. We're currently working on the Health data sync enabling for future updates.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We don't track what you drink or when. Your health data only stays on your phone.

How do I contact support?

Go to Settings > Contact in the app to send us an email.

Can I change the measurement units?

Yes, you can toggle between mg (milligrams) and other units, or change liquid measurements between ml and oz in Settings.
