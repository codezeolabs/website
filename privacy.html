Privacy Policy

At Submia, your privacy is our top priority. You stay in full control of your data at all times.

Data Security & Transparency

All your data is securely stored on your device. We do not collect, access, or transmit your personal information outside your device. No tracking, analytics, or fingerprinting is used.

What Information Do We Store?

  • Subscription info you add: name, amount, category, renewal date
  • App preferences: currency, theme, notification settings
  • No payment info, contacts, or location is collected

No sensitive or personally identifiable information (PII) is ever collected or stored.

Your Rights & Control

You can edit, export, or delete your data at any time. The Delete Data feature in Settings removes all your data, including any backups, instantly from your device.

Third-Party Services & Exceptions

No analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs. When you use the in-app Support form, only the information you provide (email, name, message) is sent securely via Web3Forms for support purposes only.

Children’s Privacy

Submia is not designed for children under 13. No data is knowingly collected from children.

App Permissions

Submia does not access your camera, contacts, photos, or location. Only notification permission is requested if you enable reminders.

Policy Updates

If our privacy policy changes, you will be notified within the app before updates take effect.

Contact Us

Questions? Contact us anytime via the Support section. We are committed to responding to all privacy inquiries.
