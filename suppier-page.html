FAQ

What does Suppier actually do?

Suppier scans supplement labels and shows you an AI-generated overview, a supplement score, benefits, effects, and an ingredient breakdown — designed to help you better understand what's inside a product.

Is Suppier medical advice?

No. Suppier is for informational purposes only and is not medical advice. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making health decisions.

How accurate is the analysis?

Suppier uses AI to interpret labels and ingredients. Results may vary depending on label quality and product. Always double-check the original label and consult a healthcare professional for personal guidance.

How does the AI supplement score work?

The score is a summary signal generated by AI based on the label content. It is not a medical rating and should not be treated as one.

Does Suppier work offline?

Scanning and analysis features may require an internet connection so the AI can analyze the label.

Is my data private?

We take privacy seriously. See our Privacy Policy for full details on what is and isn't collected.

Can Suppier read any supplement label?

Suppier works best with clear, well-lit labels in supported languages. Damaged, low-contrast, or partially visible labels may not produce reliable results.

Visit the Support page and send us a message. We usually reply within 24 hours.
