Privacy Policy

At CoffZeo, your health data belongs to you. We do not track, sell, or store your caffeine logs on our servers.

All your data is securely stored on your device. We do not collect, access, or transmit your personal health information outside your device. No tracking, analytics, or fingerprinting is used.

What Information Do We Store?

  • Caffeine logs you add: drink type, caffeine amount (mg), time of consumption
  • Metabolic settings: your weight and caffeine sensitivity (used for half-life calculations only)
  • App preferences: daily limits, notification times, theme

No sensitive or personally identifiable information (PII) is sent to CodeZeo servers.

Apple Health (HealthKit) Integration

CoffZeo integrates with Apple Health to sync your caffeine intake. This integration is optional and requires your explicit permission.

  • Read Data: We do not read other health data from your device unless necessary for caffeine analysis (e.g. sleep analysis if supported).
  • Write Data: We write your caffeine intake directly to Apple Health so you can view it alongside your other nutrition data.
  • Privacy: HealthKit data remains on your device and is not shared with us or third parties.

Your Rights & Control

You can edit, export, or delete your data at any time. The Delete Data feature in Settings removes all your logs and preferences instantly from your device.

Third-Party Services & Exceptions

No analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs. When you use the in-app Support form, only the information you provide (email, name, message) is sent securely via Web3Forms for support purposes only.

Children’s Privacy

CoffZeo is not designed for children under 13. No data is knowingly collected from children.

App Permissions

  • Notifications: To alert you when your caffeine levels are safe for sleep or when you exceed your daily limit.
  • Apple Health: To read/write caffeine data (optional).

Policy Updates

If our privacy policy changes, you will be notified within the app before updates take effect.

Contact Us

Questions? Contact us anytime via the Support section within the app or email us at info@codezeo.com. We are committed to responding to all privacy inquiries.
