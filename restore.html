Restore Purchases

If you previously bought Submia Pro, you can restore your purchase on the same Apple ID. No login required.

Purchase restoration is done inside the iOS app and handled securely by the App Store.

Open Submia & Restore

How to Restore (iPhone / iPad)

  1. Open Submia on your device.
  2. Go to Settings → Restore Purchases.
  3. Confirm with your Apple ID if asked.

Tip: Make sure you’re signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase Submia Pro.

Troubleshooting

  • Restart the app and try again.
  • Ensure your device has internet access.
  • Check that your Apple ID matches the original purchase.

If the issue persists, contact us at info@codezeo.com. We usually reply within 24 hours.
